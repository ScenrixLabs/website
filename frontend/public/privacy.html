Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026 · Last updated: June 28, 2026

Scenrix ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at scenrix.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Email address — when you submit your email to join the waitlist.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as pages visited and approximate geographic region, collected via privacy-respecting tools.
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, and IP address collected automatically by our servers.

We do NOT collect payment information at this stage.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you when your early access spot opens.
  • To send product updates and announcements (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To improve and develop our product based on aggregate usage patterns.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your email address is stored in a secure, encrypted PostgreSQL database hosted on a VPS located in the EU. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@scenrix.com.

5. Cookies

We use no tracking cookies. We may use functional browser storage (localStorage) to improve your experience on the site.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts for typography. Google's privacy policy applies to data transferred to their servers for font loading. We do not use Google Analytics or any other third-party advertising platforms.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify waitlist members of any material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@scenrix.com.
